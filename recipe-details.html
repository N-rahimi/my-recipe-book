Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Chicken or mutton - 500g
  • Onion - 1 large, thinly sliced
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Garam masala - 2 teaspoons
  • Saffron - a pinch (soaked in warm water)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Coriander leaves - for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fry the onions until golden and set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken or mutton with yogurt, garam masala, garlic, ginger, and salt for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice in boiling water with a pinch of saffron. Drain when done.
  4. Layer the marinated meat with rice in a large pot. Garnish with fried onions and coriander leaves.
  5. Cover and cook for 20-30 minutes on low heat until the meat is tender.

  • Rice - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Dill - 2 tablespoons
  • Pine nuts - 1/4 cup
  • Ground beef (optional) - 250g
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons

Instructions:

  1. Soften the grape leaves in warm water.

  3. Roll the mixture in the grape leaves tightly, then cook them in a pot with water and a drizzle of olive oil for 30 minutes.

Pad Thai

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles - 200g
  • Shrimp - 200g
  • Eggs - 2, beaten
  • Peanuts - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Fish sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Spring onions - 2, chopped
  • Lime - 1, cut into wedges
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water until soft, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat some oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until fragrant. Add the shrimp and cook until pink.
  3. Add the beaten eggs and scramble. Once cooked, add the rice noodles, fish sauce, sugar, and mix well.
  4. Top with peanuts, spring onions, and coriander leaves. Serve with lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor.

Borek

  • Onions - 1 large, finely chopped

Tropical Fruit Tart

Ingredients:

  • Tart dough - 1 package (store-bought or homemade)
  • Heavy cream - 200 ml
  • Mascarpone cheese - 250g
  • Mango - 1, sliced
  • Kiwi - 2, sliced
  • Pineapple - 1 cup, diced
  • Passion fruit - 1 (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Bake the tart shell according to the package instructions and let it cool.
  2. Whip the heavy cream and mix it with mascarpone cheese until smooth. Fill the tart with the cream mixture.
  3. Arrange the tropical fruits (mango, kiwi, pineapple) on top. Drizzle with passion fruit pulp before serving.

Mixed Berry Juice

Ingredients:

  • Sponge cake - 1, cut into layers
  • Fresh strawberries - 1 pint, sliced
  • Whipped cream - 1 cup, sweetened
  • Sugar - 1 tablespoon (for strawberries)

Instructions:

  1. Whip the cream with a tablespoon of sugar until stiff peaks form.
  2. Layer the sponge cake with whipped cream and sliced strawberries.
  3. Top with more cream and strawberries for garnish. Chill before serving.

Mango Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • Mango - 1, peeled and chopped
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Honey - 1 tablespoon
  • Ice cubes - 1/2 cup

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together until smooth. Add more honey or yogurt if needed.
  2. Serve chilled in a glass and enjoy!

Mixed Berry Juice

Ingredients:

  • Fresh strawberries - 1 cup
  • Raspberries - 1/2 cup
  • Blueberries - 1/2 cup
  • Fresh orange juice - 1 cup
  • Honey or sugar - as desired
  • Ice - as needed

Instructions:

  1. Wash all the berries and place them in a blender.
  2. Add orange juice and honey.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Serve in a glass with ice.
